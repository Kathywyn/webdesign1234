-About What Am I Doing These Days-

Every morning, I wake up at 7:15 a.m.

I go to school by school bus at 8:00 a.m.

I have breakfast at 8:30 a.m.

Then I have a lesson about
web design at 9:00 a.m.

At noon, I enjoy my lunch at 12:00.

At 1:00 p.m, it's time for another lesson.

I have dinner at 5:00 p.m.
It is a little bit too early for
me to have dinner at that time.

It's time for some activities for
me and my classmates at 6:00 p.m.
(ps: Actually we have chairs to sit
on during activities but it is too
difficult to draw so I didn't draw it. lol)

I return to the dorm by school bus at 8:00 p.m.

It's the end of the day and I go to bed at 11:30 p.m.
